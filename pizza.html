Pizza

Here are the ingredients you will need to make it:

  • tomato sauces
  • pepperoni
  • shredded mozzarella

Here are the steps to make the pizza:

  1. Preheat your oven
  2. Prepare the dough
  3. Place sauce onto the dough evenly
  4. Spread shredded cheese evenly on top of the sauce
  5. Place the pepperoni on top
